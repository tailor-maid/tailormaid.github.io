Cookie policy

Cookies perform essential functions, such as ensuring a webpage loads correctly and securely. They also help us provide you with a consistent and efficient experience. For example, to remember that you are a repeat visitor so we can direct you to the appropriate part of our Websites or not challenge you again, or to present you with services that are matched to your preferences.

What's a cookie?

Cookies are small pieces of text used to store information on web browsers. They are used by many websites to store and receive identifiers and other information on devices, such as a handheld phone or computer. You can learn more about cookies and their functions generally by visiting an information website such as allaboutcookies.org.

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default, however, you may be able to delete cookies yourself through your browserâ€™s cookie manager.
